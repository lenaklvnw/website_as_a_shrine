you're here now. be my guest.



this is where i keep a part of myself everyday.
i take this little project as an opportunity
to convey some of what wasn't long ago here.
no intimate or personal thoughts though,
just snippets that emerged from day to day
that were written at the very same place most of the time.



for this current period in my life, i'll call it:
